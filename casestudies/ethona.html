The Opportunity

Define Ethona's Voice

For Ethona, AUX defined Ethona's Voice, and created a package of uniquely branded audio assets designed to live alongside existing branding and marketing efforts. By considering competitor positioning and assessing unique Ethona brand characteristics, AUX implemented a holistic system to differentiate, emotionally engage, and imporve brand awareness through audio user experience.

Market Audit

When defining a brand's audio aesthetic, our team starts with a comprehensive analysis of both the brand and the competition. We look at where and how their competitors may be using sound to carve out a unique market position for the brand.

In Ethona's case, we discovered that over 60% of their competitors were utilizing a generic EDM inspired aesthetic to position themselves as youthful and uplifting. The voice we set out to define would avoid this stereotypical aesthetic.

Genres

The Ethona Audio Aesthetic borrows from styles like Future Bass, Chillwave, Tropical House and UK Garage, while acknowledging a Pop ethos. Nuanced ambient passages diversify its overall presence to provide creative depth.
