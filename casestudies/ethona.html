ETHONA

The Opportunity

Define Ethona's Voice

For active lifestyle apparel brand Ethona, AUX defined Ethona's voice, and created a package of uniquely branded audio assets designed to live alongside existing branding and marketing efforts. By considering competitor positioning and assessing Ethona’s unique brand characteristics, AUX implemented a holistic system to differentiate, emotionally engage, and improve brand awareness through audio user experience.

Market Audit

When defining a brand's audio aesthetic, our team starts with a comprehensive analysis of both the brand and the competition. We look at where and how their competitors may be using sound to carve out a unique market position for the brand.

In Ethona's case, we discovered that over 60% of their competitors were utilizing a generic EDM inspired aesthetic to position themselves as youthful and uplifting. The voice we set out to define would avoid this stereotypical sound.

EBUs

AUX has created a set of extractable branding units that define Ethona’s unique audio aesthetic. These smaller, more versatile elements are built to permeate through all Ethona audio assets.

Genres

The Ethona audio aesthetic borrows from styles like future bass, chillwave, tropical house and UK garage, while acknowledging a pop ethos. Nuanced ambient passages diversify its overall presence to provide creative depth.

About The Ethona Lead

The Ethona Lead is a melodic passage that represents the Ethona woman's desire to improve and strive for more. It’s melody is written to work over a wide array of possible chord progressions, making it easy to recognize without ever becoming stale.

Examples in Assets
About The Ethona Motif

The Ethona Motif is simple, powerful, and one of the cornerstones of Ethona’s audio aesthetic. Its melodic structure and female vocal-based timbre connect back to Ethona’s mission statement: inspiring women to fight for more and motivating others in the process.

Examples in Assets
About Glass Percussion

Glass Percussion echoes the shimmering essence of a dream(er). In up-tempo passages, glass percussion gives the story energy and grabs the attention of the audience. In softer, more emotive passages, it appears as glistening textures to give the story depth.

Examples in Assets
About Vocal Echoes

Airy Vocals speak to the feminine and empowering nature of the Ethona brand. This is the voice in your head telling you to keep going. The examples show how Ethona can use distinctly branded vocal echoes for varying energy levels and emotional contexts.

Examples in Assets
About Physical Drums

Physical Drums blend live organic elements with modern electronic elements. Constructed to feel kickass without being aggressive, the emotional vibe they create represents a journey of self improvement. The use of worldly elements represents Ethona’s cosmopolitan mindset and values of giving back to the community.

Spotify Playlist

The AUX team helped Ethona create a Spotify playlist of 100 songs that aligns with the established audio aesthetic. Each song contains elements that mirror the EBUs and instruments, such as female vocals, worldly percussion, metallic instruments, sampled voices, and lyrical content communicating Ethona's message of self empowerment.
