About Us

We are the next evolution of audio branding /

Who We Are

We are a creative audio agency that believes in sound's ability to evoke passion, loyalty, and inspiration.

What We Do

We identify how people interact with your story and turn those moments into emotionally charged memorable experiences. Together, those branded moments create your audio user experience.

Our Services

Across every medium, platform, and experience, we create inspiring audio that feels intentional.


Branding

We start from scratch to build your brand a modern, thoughtful, and emotionally driven audio aesthetic. The audio aesthetic style guide we create allows us to easily extend your brand's voice across your unique experiences.

Multimedia

Our team provides original music composition, sound design, and post production services for film, TV, advertising and video games. Our passion and experience allow us to give your story the impact it deserves.

Interactive

As users, we understand the effect good sound design can have on the overall user experience of a product. It's not just important for the sound to be interesting, exciting, or unique; but equally important for the sound to make sense from a brand and interaction standpoint.
